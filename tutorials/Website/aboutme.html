About Me

My motivation and process

My name is Alan Joseph. I am 21 years old and I am a third-year college student attending San Jose State University. I am currently pursuing a bachelor's in fine arts with a concentration in digital media as well as a minor in Interaction Design.

Thoughout my life, I have experienced working with many different mediums of digital media. In my childhood, I enjoyed illustration, character design, and more traditional mediums. However, as I grew older, I found myself pursuing digital art and growing more interested in graphic design and branding. And now, as a college student, I have found a passion for UI/UX design and web design. And so throughout my journey, I have developed niches for myself in various areas of digital media. For the longest time I thought this was a bad thing, as I felt like I couldn't specialize in anything. But now I realize that having a wide variety of skills and experiences is a strength in itself, as it allows me to adapt to different situations and challenges with ease. I can put all my skills together in order to be adaptable and versatile in the field of digital media. With this mindset, I have come to consider myself a "digital swiss army knife", as I can take on many different roles and tasks.

I am still exploring and learning more about the field of digital media and design as a whole, but I am excited to see where this journey takes me. I hope that I can continue to develop new skills and hone my existing ones, and that I can use my talents to create meaningful and impactful work!
