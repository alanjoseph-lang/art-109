Exercise 4

Bioluminescent Jellyfish

Description:

For this exercise, I had to turn my original jellyfish into a class and make atleast 15 of them on the canvas. Because of this, I decided to do a bioluminiscent deep sea theme. I randomized the colors and sizes, added opacity, and tweaked the trignometric animations of the base model to better fit this theme. For my interactions, I wanted something that would be on theme with the concept, so I decided to play with the rotation speeds as well as make the jellyfish move away if you clicked near them. I also created a glow pulse effect that shows up whenever the jellyfish are reacting to the effects.

  • Click to push the Jellyfish
  • Press R to make them rotate faster
  • Reload to randomize colors
