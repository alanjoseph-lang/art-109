Questions

Answer the questions. The first letter of each correct answer will form your password when arranged by question order. Once you have the password, you can press the interactive square button and it will take you to the Vigenere Square tool in a new tab. You can use this to help you decode the message. When you are done, type your answer into the field at the bottom of the box and submit to see if you are correct

*Hack: one of the question answers is the password itself. If you want to test your luck/intuition, you could potentially get the password without answering all the questions
