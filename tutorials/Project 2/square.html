Vigenere Square Tool

Top Row = Password Letter

Left Column = Encoded Message Letter

Intersecting Cell = Decoded Letter

Example: if your encoded message was XANBK and your password was KEY, find K on the top row and X on the left column. Next is E (top) and A (left), then Y (top) and N (left)

Since the passcode in the example is shorter than the message, you loop back look for K (top) and B (left), and then E (top) and K (left)

This should decode the message as HELLO

P.S. just hover over the letters and the row and column will be highlighted
