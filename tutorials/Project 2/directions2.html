Puzzle

You will be given a message encoded in the Vigenere Cipher. You must answer a series of questions to find out the passcode. The first letter of each answer will sequentially form the password needed to decode the message.
Once you have the passcode, decode the message and enter it as your answer. To make things simpler, an interactive vigenere square will be provided that just requires you to hover over the letters. Good luck!
