Cat

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility, playful behavior, and ability to hunt vermin.

Dog

Dogs are domesticated mammals that have been bred for various purposes, including companionship, work, and protection. They are known for their loyalty and intelligence.

Owl

Owls are nocturnal birds of prey known for their distinctive hooting calls and exceptional night vision. They play a crucial role in controlling rodent populations.
